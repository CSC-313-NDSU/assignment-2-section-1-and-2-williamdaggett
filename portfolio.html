My Job - National Guard

I'm a Guardsman with the North Dakota Air National Guard. I work there as a Command and Control Operations Specialist, which means I manage the Command Post. For non-military folks, that means I manage information distribution around the base, especially for emergency management situations, but since emergencies don't happen all too often, a lot of it is sitting in a basement with no windows, studying for and being prepared for something to happen.

Kickboxing

My biggest passion is kickboxing! I'm an amateur kickboxer with a record of 0 wins and 2 losses in sanctioned bouts, and 1 win by TKO and 0 knockouts in a smoker (a non-sanctioned bout meant to simulate one). I'm now an officer for the NDSU Martial Arts and Boxing Club where I regularly teach kickboxing classes!

Drone Pilot

I'm a certified drone pilot through FAA Part 107. This means I can legally own higher-weight drones than normal and can fly in more airspace than is standard. Enforcing these regulations is extremely tough, and many don't even know they're in effect, so if you're unlicensed, please do not fly a drone weighing more than 250g without registering it and never fly a drone above 50ft AGL. I've been working on my FPV skills and have (most of) the parts to build my own, so stay tuned for that.

Basic Life Support Instructor

I'm a First Aid/CPR/Basic Life Support Instructor through the American Heart Association! I decided I didn't want to pursue emergency medicine as a career but I am allowed to teach others the basics. If you notice an extra bag hanging from my backpack, it's probably my medical kit. Be careful asking about it, because I will spend as long as you let me talking about its layout and contents.

SCUBA Diving

I'm a PADI Certified Open Water SCUBA Diver. I don't get to practice that skillset much in this neck of the woods, but it's still fun to talk about.
